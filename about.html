HOW TO MAKE FRIED BANANA

  • Introduction :

    • Pisang goreng is a snack that is widely found in Indonesia, Malaysia and Singapore. This food is made from bananas, after peeling the skin, cut into pieces as desired and then covered with a thick dough made from a mixture of flour, a little salt and sugar, then fried in hot oil.

      Purpose :

    Knowing how to make fried bananas, not just buying them & Knowing the ingredients, tools, and step by step how to make cheese fried banana food.

      How to make:
    - Ingredient :
      1. Banana Candle
      2. 6 tablespoons of flour
      3. Rice flour 3 tablespoons
      4. Sugar 3 spoons
      5. Salt to taste
      6. Baking powder 1/4 spoon
      7. enough water
      8. Additional cheese as topping
      9. Margarine
      10. Cooking Oil

        

      - Ways of making :
      1. Prepare materials and what is needed.
      2. Put 6 spoons of flour and 3 spoons of rice flour into a bowl.
      3. Enter 3 tablespoons of sugar and salt to taste.
      4. Add 1/4 of baking powder.
      5. Add enough water.
      1. Stir the dough until dissolved.
      2. Then give a little margarine and stir again.
      3. Then peel and cut the bananas.
      4. Dip the sliced banana into the batter.
      1. Prepare a skillet/pan with hot oil.
      2. Then fry the bananas covered with the batter into the skillet.
      3. Serve the bananas on a plate, with the grated cheese.
